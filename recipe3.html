Grilled Salmon with Avocado Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mash the avocados and mix with minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, cilantro, salt, and pepper. Set the avocado sauce aside.
  3. Brush the salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the salmon fillets on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  5. Serve the grilled salmon with the avocado sauce on top.
