Chicken Tacos with Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast strips
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (opcional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 corn or flour tortillas

For the yogurt sauce:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, ground cumin, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Marinate the chicken strips in this mixture for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  3. Grill the chicken strips for about 5-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a skillet or in the oven.
  5. To make the yogurt sauce, combine plain yogurt, grated cucumber, chopped fresh mint, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  6. Fill each tortilla with grilled chicken and drizzle with yogurt sauce.
