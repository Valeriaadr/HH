Zucchini and Pesto Chicken Roll-Ups

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce (traditional or basil pesto)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for greasing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash the zucchinis and cut them into long, thin strips using a mandoline or vegetable peeler. These strips will be used as "wraps" for the roll-ups.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the shredded chicken with pesto sauce, halved cherry tomatoes, and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place a spoonful of the chicken and pesto mixture at one end of a zucchini strip and roll it up to create a roll-up. Repeat this process with all the zucchini strips.
  5. Grease a baking dish with olive oil. Arrange the zucchini roll-ups in the dish, seam side down.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining grated Parmesan cheese on top of the roll-ups.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 15-20 minutes or until the zucchini is tender, and the cheese is golden brown.
  8. Serve the zucchini and pesto chicken roll-ups as a healthy and delicious meal.
