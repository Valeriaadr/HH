Quinoa and Roasted Vegetable Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 eggplant, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Rinse quinoa in a fine-mesh strainer under cold running water. Combine quinoa and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until quinoa is tender and water is absorbed. Let cool.1
  2. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the red and yellow bell peppers, zucchini, and eggplant on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until tender and slightly golden.
  3. 3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and roasted vegetables. Squeeze lemon juice over the mixture and add cilantro and chopped walnuts. Stir well and serve.
