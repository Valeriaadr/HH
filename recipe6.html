Fruit and Spinach Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or your preferred milk)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (opcional)
  • Ice cubes (opcional)

Instructions:

  1. Place fresh spinach, ripe banana, frozen strawberries, Greek yogurt, almond milk, honey (if desired), and ice cubes (if desired) in a blender.
  2. Blend all the ingredients until you have a smooth and creamy smoothie.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy this refreshing and healthy drink.
